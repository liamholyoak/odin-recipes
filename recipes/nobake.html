Easy No Bake Cookies

No-Bake Cookies made with instant chocolate milk mix (not baking cocoa).

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup powdered chocolate drink mix

Directions

  1. In a medium saucepan, over medium heat, combine the sugar, salt, butter and milk.
  2. Bring to a boil, then boil for 1 full minute.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in the peanut butter, oats, vanilla and chocolate drink mix
  4. Drop by spoonfuls onto waxed paper.
  5. Let stand until completely cool.
